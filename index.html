Privacy Policy

This page explains how Gym Trainer App collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the platform as a client, coach, or administrator. It is written to read like a real app policy, not a placeholder.

Effective April 19, 2026 Updated for the current release Designed for fitness coaching workflows
What we collect
Account data, workouts, progress entries, media, device signals, and diagnostic logs.
How we use it
To run the service, authenticate users, manage workouts, deliver notifications, and secure accounts.
What we do not do
We do not sell personal data and we do not use it for unrelated advertising.

At a glance

Gym Trainer App is built to help trainers and clients manage workouts, progress, feedback, media, and communication. Because that requires account data and some technical information, the policy below explains exactly what is collected and why.

Data you enter

Profile details, workout plans, progress notes, uploads, and messages you choose to store in the app.

Data we generate

Session, device, diagnostic, and notification data used for secure operation and troubleshooting.

Data handling promise

We keep information only as long as needed for service delivery, legal compliance, and security.

Section 01

Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly when you create an account, use training features, upload media, or communicate with the Gym Trainer App team. This can include your name, email address, role selection, password, workout plans, progress updates, feedback, notes, and any content you choose to send through the app.

We may also collect technical and usage information automatically when you use the app. That can include device type, operating system, app version, approximate IP address, crash logs, push notification tokens, and diagnostic data that helps us keep the service stable.

  • Account details such as your name, email address, password, and user role.
  • Fitness and coaching content that you enter, upload, or store in the app.
  • Device, session, and performance data used for security, debugging, and product improvement.
Section 02

How we use information

We use your information to create and manage your account, authenticate sign-in attempts, deliver workouts and client data, enable media uploads, send notifications, and support the core coaching features of Gym Trainer App.

We also use information to improve the app, detect bugs or abuse, protect accounts from unauthorized access, respond to support requests, and comply with legal obligations. If you contact support, we may retain the conversation so we can resolve your issue and avoid repeating work later.

  • Provide the app’s workout, client, media, and progress-tracking features.
  • Authenticate users, verify accounts, and protect the platform from misuse.
  • Monitor performance and fix technical issues without exposing unnecessary personal data.
Section 03

Legal bases and consent

Where applicable law requires a legal basis, we rely on performance of a contract when we provide the service you requested, legitimate interests when we secure and improve the platform, consent when we ask for permission for specific features, and legal obligation when we need to keep records or respond to lawful requests.

If you give us consent for optional features, you can withdraw that consent at any time through your device settings, account settings, or by contacting support. Withdrawing consent will not affect processing that already happened before you changed your preference.

Section 04

How information is shared

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data when necessary to run the service, protect users, or comply with the law.

Depending on how you use the app, information may be shared with service providers that help us host the app, store data, send push notifications, process authentication, or manage file uploads. We may also share information if we are required to do so by law, to enforce our terms, to investigate fraud or security incidents, or to protect the rights and safety of users and the public.

  • With trusted infrastructure or support providers that operate parts of the service on our behalf.
  • With coaches or clients only when the app’s collaboration features are used and the sharing is part of the product design.
  • With legal, regulatory, or law enforcement authorities when required by applicable law.
Section 05

Storage and security

We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. These safeguards include access controls, limited internal access, and secure transmission practices where supported by the platform.

No system is perfectly secure. If a security issue ever affects your data, we will investigate the incident, take steps to contain it, and notify affected users when required by law.

Section 06

Retention and deletion

We keep your information for as long as needed to provide Gym Trainer App, maintain account history, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When information is no longer needed, we delete it or anonymize it within a reasonable timeframe.

If you request deletion of your account or content, we will take reasonable steps to remove or de-identify the data from active systems unless retention is required by law, fraud prevention, security, or legitimate business recordkeeping.

Section 07

Your rights and choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to certain processing of your personal information. You may also have the right to receive a copy of your data or request that we transfer it to another service when technically feasible.

You can also manage some information directly through the app, update your profile, control notifications on your device, or remove content you no longer want stored. If a setting is not available in the app, you can contact support for help.

  • Review and update your profile information where the app allows it.
  • Control push notification preferences through your device settings.
  • Ask for access, correction, or deletion through the official support channel.
Section 08

Children’s privacy

The app is not intended for children under the age required by local law to provide consent for online services, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children who are not permitted to use the service. If we learn that a child provided information without appropriate consent, we will take steps to delete it as required.

Section 09

Third-party services

The app may contain integrations or links to services we do not control. Those third parties have their own privacy practices, and we recommend reviewing their policies before enabling the integration or sharing information with them. Examples may include authentication tools, cloud storage providers, notification services, analytics tools, or media delivery infrastructure.

Section 10

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in Gym Trainer App, legal requirements, or our data practices. When we make material changes, we will update the effective date on this page and, where appropriate, provide additional notice in the app or by email.

Plain-language summary

In simple terms, we collect the information required to run the coaching app, protect accounts, and make the experience work reliably. We only share it with service providers or other users when the product feature requires it, and we keep it only as long as needed.

For clients Your workouts, progress, and media are used to track performance and collaborate with your coach.
For coaches Client data is used to manage assignments, feedback, and training oversight inside the app.
For everyone Technical data helps us secure accounts, fix bugs, and keep the service running smoothly.

Contact and updates

If you have a privacy question, want to exercise a data right, or need help understanding how your information is handled, contact the Gym Trainer App team through the in-app support flow or the official support details associated with your deployment of the app.

We recommend keeping this policy visible from your app’s login or settings area so users can review it before creating an account or connecting with a coach.
